Hardwood Flooring Services

From brand‑new installs to full refinishing and stairs, Frady's Flooring treats every job like it’s their own home.

Hardwood Flooring Installation

Tear out old floors and replace them with solid, properly installed hardwood that’s straight, tight, and built to last.

  • Careful subfloor prep to avoid squeaks and soft spots.
  • Precise layout so boards run clean and straight.
  • Finish options to match your home and lifestyle.

Hardwood Refinishing

Turn scratched, dull floors into rich, smooth hardwood again with professional sanding and fresh finish.

  • Dust‑controlled sanding where possible.
  • Color and sheen matched to your home.
  • Repairs for minor gaps, cracks, and damage.

Stairs & Custom Woodwork

Stairs, landings, lofts, and rail transitions finished to look like they were always part of the house.

  • Custom stair treads and nosing.
  • Clean transitions between rooms and levels.
  • Detail work around posts, rails, and trim.

Tile & LVP (When You Need It)

For kitchens, baths, and basements that need something other than hardwood, Frady's can also install quality tile and LVP.

  • Tile layout that avoids slivers and awkward cuts.
  • LVP installed flat and tight with clean seams.
  • Grout and trim chosen to match your space.

Floors That Feel Solid Underfoot

You notice good hardwood every time you walk across it. Tight seams, no soft spots, and a smooth, even feel from room to room.

Lines That Stay Tight

Careful layout and prep keep your boards running straight so seams stay tight instead of opening up over time.

  • Straight runs in long rooms.
  • Tight joints at walls and doorways.
  • No random gaps or wavy lines.

Stairs That Feel Solid

Solid treads, clean nosing, and tight risers so every step feels sturdy, not hollow or uneven.

  • Custom‑fit treads and risers.
  • No squeaks or spongy steps.
  • Safe edges that feel comfortable underfoot.
